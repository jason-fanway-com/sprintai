You fill out an onboarding form. Tell us your business name, what you do, your hours, your services, pricing, FAQs, whatever you want it to know. We load that into your AI's knowledge base. The more detail you give, the better the answers. Most people are up and running in under 30 minutes.

Do I need to change my phone number? +

No. You keep your existing number for calls. We give you a separate dedicated business text number that your AI handles. You can put it on your website, your Google Business Profile, your social media. "Text us at (xxx) xxx-xxxx." That goes straight to your AI.

Can I see what the AI is saying to my customers? +

Yes. You get a dashboard with every conversation. Every chat, every text thread, everything the AI said and everything the customer asked. You can jump in and take over any conversation at any time if you want to handle it personally.

What if the AI can't answer something? +

It's honest about it. If a question is outside what it knows, it tells the customer something like "I don't have that info handy, but you can call us at [your number] or I can have someone follow up with you." It never makes things up. It escalates cleanly when needed.

How fast can I get set up? +

Fast. Fill out the onboarding form, we train your AI, you get the chat widget code and your SMS number. Most businesses are live within 24-48 hours of signing up. Some the same day. There's no technical skill required on your end.

Can I cancel anytime? +

Yes. Month-to-month. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no "please speak to our retention team." If it's not working for you, you cancel. That's it. We think you'll stay because the results speak for themselves, not because we made it hard to leave.
