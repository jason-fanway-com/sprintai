What makes this different

Customers don't download anything. They don't make an account. They don't remember a password. They text a number. That's the whole experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I need new hardware? +
No. A phone for the merchant dashboard and whatever you already use for tickets. That's it.
Do I have to change my POS or payment processor? +
No. SprintAI sends payment links through a hosted checkout. Funds go to your account like any other online payment.
What if we run out of something? +
Tap it sold out in the merchant view. The system stops offering it until the next morning, when it resets automatically. You can also text "OUT everything" from an authorized staff phone.
What if something breaks mid-morning? +
There's a big red "pause ordering" button in your merchant view. One tap and new orders stop coming in until you're ready.
Do you deliver? +
Pickup only for now. The simplest version of the product has to be great before we add anything else.
How much does it cost? +
Our first pilot shops are on the house while we build alongside them. Pricing for shop #4 and beyond is in development; we'll be upfront about it before we ask anyone to pay.
Who's behind this? +
A small team out of the Lehigh Valley. Background in enterprise software, restaurant customer experience, and actual texting of pizza shops.
